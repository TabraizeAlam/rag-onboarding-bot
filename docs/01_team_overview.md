## Communication Norms

- **Slack** is the primary async channel. Response SLA is 4 hours during business hours.
- **Email** is for external stakeholders and formal announcements only.
- **Zoom** for any meeting with 3+ people.
- Do not DM for technical questions — use the appropriate team Slack channel so knowledge is shared.
- Tag `@here` only for genuinely urgent issues. Use `@channel` only for emergencies.

## Team Principles

1. **Ship small, ship often.** PRs should be reviewable in under 30 minutes.
2. **Docs are code.** Every feature ships with updated documentation.
3. **Blameless postmortems.** Incidents are learning opportunities, not blame assignments.
4. **Default to async.** Respect focus time; schedule meetings only when synchronous discussion is necessary.
